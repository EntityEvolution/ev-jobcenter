Rules

Basic Concepts

1.1 | OOC

All actions that occur outside of the role.

1.2 | IC

All actions that occur within the role.

1.3 | MG

Occurs when any type of out-of-game information is used in-game, either intentionally or by mistake, for profit. It is prohibited and carries a penalty.

1.4 | PG

It is being able to do things that in real life could not be done, because they do not make sense or logic or because they are impossible that can be done here because it is a game. These types of actions are sanctioned.

1.5 | /me

It is used to describe actions that do not have animation within the game. They should be written in the third person (/ me: he takes the notebook out of his pocket).

1.6 | /do

It is used to describe the environment, feelings and states. You should always tell the truth (/ do you have a pulse?). It is forbidden to use the / do in a forced way, for example: / do Would it be true? and then interpret the role differently since you have read it but you could not know it because it would be considered MG. It is forbidden to lie for / do

1.7 | Environment

We must remember that within the game there are people on the street and they may be watching what we do.

1.8 | Value of life

The most important IC rule, you will have to always seek the survival of your character above anything else.

Killing

2.1 | VDM

It is killing another player using a vehicle as a weapon. These types of actions are sanctioned.

2.2 | PK

It occurs when our character dies non-definitively and is revived by an EMS. It involves a loss of memory from the previous role.

2.3 | PKT

It involves the loss of memory of your character's entire life, including your friends, groups, work ... It does not entail a loss of assets.

2.4 | CK

It is when our character dies permanently, including the loss of money, weapons and identity (name).

2.5 | RK

When a player ignores the memory loss role explained above (PK) and goes to the place where he died to retaliate against his killer. It is prohibited and carries a penalty.

2.6 | DM

Player who is dedicated to attacking or killing other players for no apparent or prior reason. It is prohibited and carries a penalty.

Jobs

POLICE

1.1 | Articles

Article 1

Help and protect people and ensure the conservation and custody of assets that are in danger from any cause.

Article 2

Monitor and protect public buildings and facilities that require it.

Article 3

Maintain and restore, where appropriate, public order and security.

Article 4

Prevent criminal acts.

Article 5

Faced with the lack of respect of 1 civilian, 2 notices will be given, if this persists, you will be stopped reading her rights. In the event that he will use violence, the taser can be used to defend himself and above all not to abuse it.

Article 6

It is totally forbidden to abuse your power by breaking all traffic regulations. You can only do it if you are in code 3.

Article 7

It is forbidden to take a friend or whoever you want while you are on duty in your official car.

Article 8

When out of service, all weapons must be left in the armory, except for the regulation pistol, which is always mandatory.

Article 9

When detaining a person it is important to cut off communications and if you need a call, return the phone at the police station.

Article 10

All weapons are illegal even if they carry a license, this only allows them to carry a pistol. If he were to use it, it would be confiscated for using a weapon to carry out criminal acts, if the person wants the weapon to be returned to him, he must file an appeal with his lawyer and send the papers for the purchase of said item.

Article 11

It is totally forbidden to disrespect 1 partner and / or civilian.

Article 12

It is totally forbidden to leave official vehicles abandoned, you should always call the mechanic.

Article 13

Police officers or other lower ranks may be H50, if there is no one higher than them.

Article 14

The person in charge of negotiating will be a policeman dressed in a suit with a vest. Authorized to negotiate for the command available at that time. If 4 agents attend a robbery. The negotiator will not count as a police officer and must go if the colleagues request it. If they break up the negotiations, he will be able to join the shooting.
